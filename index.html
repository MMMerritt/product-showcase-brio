Our Story

- noun: vigor or vivacity of style or performance; energy; spirit; liveliness

At Brio Coffee, we are passionate and committed to bringing that vigor, spirit, and warmth into each cup you brew for you and your loved ones. We are a small-batch roastery based in Houston, Texas, and we work closely with farmers all over the world to achieve the best quality organic beans that will truly satisfy a wide range of palettes.

Our goal with each of our roasts is to provide coffee that is balanced and fully-flavored. Whether you need caffeine to wake up early in the morning, focus on a late-night project, or simply want a comforting treat, Brio coffee will brighten your spirit and warm your heart.

Sustainability

Great coffee requires focus, research, and love, which all begins at the source: the farms and farmers. We have established direct connections with farms to ensure that the farmers and their families who depend on the coffee trade are treated and compensated fairly. Our partners are devoted to harvesting top-quality coffee berries so that each roast embodies the complex and unique attributes of the region.

Brio Coffee is certified organic, Fair Trade, and Single Origin, originating from farms in Brazil, Kenya, and Peru. You can trust that our coffee is respectful and supportive to the environment and the farmers who work so hard.
